Featured Work

Case Studies

FinTech Dashboard React

Enterprise Financial Analytics Platform

The Problem: Financial analysts were overwhelmed by dense, unreadable spreadsheets. They needed a real-time, highly visual dashboard to track market volatility without UI lag.
My Process & Role: As the Lead Frontend Developer, I mapped out complex data flows. I designed a customized component library in Figma, ensuring charts were accessible. Development was executed using React, integrating heavy WebSocket streams.
The Challenge: Rendering thousands of data points per second caused browser freezing. I overcame this by implementing Canvas-based charting (Chart.js) and virtualized lists to keep the DOM light.
Quantifiable Impact:
  • Reduced data-load time by 1.4s via efficient state management.
  • Increased daily active user (DAU) retention by 25% due to improved UX.
EdTech SEO Next.js

Ranihati GPS Official Portal

The Problem: A government primary school lacked a centralized digital hub for parents and teachers. Crucial announcements were getting lost, and the existing system was completely inaccessible on mobile devices.
My Process & Role: I acted as the sole product designer and full-stack developer. I conducted user interviews with parents to understand their technical literacy, created high-fidelity responsive wireframes, and built the site using Next.js for superior SEO and routing.
The Challenge: Designing for strict Web Accessibility (A11y) standards while maintaining a modern, aesthetic "glassmorphism" UI. Contrast ratios had to be rigorously tested.
Quantifiable Impact:
  • Achieved 100% Lighthouse Scores in Performance, Accessibility, and SEO.
  • Adopted by 500+ parents within the first month of launch.
